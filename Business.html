What is E-Business?

E-business is the integration of information and communication technologies (ICT) to streamline all business processes, from interal management to external commercial transaction. It represents the total digital transformation of a company's value chain .

E-Commerce

  • E-commerce or electronic commerce refers specifically to the buying and selling goods or services over the internet. It focuses on the transaction itself, covering the transfer of funds and data to execute an online sale.

Trading

  • Trading is the act of buying and selling financial assets such as stocks, currencies, or commodities with the goal of generating a profit from short-term or long-term price fluctuations.

Freelancing

  • Freelancing is a professional arrangement where an individual works independently rather than being employed by a single company .

Content Creation

  • Content creation is the process of generating topic ideas and creating visuel or written assets. In a professional context, it is the fundamental engine of digital marketing, designed to build brand awareness, engage users, and drive conversions through storytelling and information sharing.

Steps to Start an Online Business

1. Idea

Choose a product or service you like and people need.

2. Research

Study your customers, their needs, and your competitors.

3. Plan

Create your roadmap: target audience, strategy, and goals.

4. Presence

Build a website or use social media (Instagram/TikTok).

5. Marketing

Use ads, influencers, and creative content to reach people.

6. Launch

Start selling, communicate with customers, and improve.
